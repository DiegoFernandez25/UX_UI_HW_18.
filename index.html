My Skills

User Research and Usability Testing

I have a strong understanding of user research and the process it takes to make if effective. I am an effective communicator both verbally and in writing and am able to run usability tests effectively in a one on one setting or virtual setting. I am able to know which tests to run and am effective in understanding who the user is.

Prototyping/Wireframing

I am good at focusing prioritizing page elements based on user needs to make the wireframing the best it can be. I am also skilled at prototyping at multiple levels and making sure it is done well to be able to run tests and gather effective feedback needed in order to improve the prototype.

Information Architecture

I am skilled at effectively organizing and structuring content whether it be through a presentation, a prototype, or a usability testing. I make it easier for the user to find where they are, locate relative information needed, find where they need to go and whats next. I am also skilled at working on projects with very bad information architecture and finding the important and relevant information.
